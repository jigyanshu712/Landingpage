IMS Engineering College,Ghaziabad


Located in the industrial city of Ghaziabad, IMS Engineering College is known for quality technical and management education, centres of excellence, innovative teaching pedagogy, discipline, and other creative efforts.
IMSEC Ghaziabad is one of the top-notch Engineering colleges in North India which has created a niche for itself since its establishment in 2002. It is ranked 5th in North zone among the top Private Engineering colleges by Times Engineering Institute Ranking Survey 2022.
